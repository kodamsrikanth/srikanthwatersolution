Product Description

The Sediment Filter is the first and essential filtration stage in RO and UV water purifiers.

It effectively removes visible impurities such as sand, dust, rust, mud, and suspended particles from the input water.

By filtering sediments at the initial stage, it protects carbon filters and RO membrane, improving overall purifier performance and lifespan.

Suitable for borewell, tanker, and municipal water sources. Ideal for domestic and small office RO systems.
