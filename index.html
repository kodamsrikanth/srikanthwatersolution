Q & A

Do you provide RO water purifier installation service?

Yes, we provide professional RO installation service for all brands. Installation charges start from ₹500 depending on the purifier model.

Which locations do you serve?

We provide RO service and sales in Godavarikhani, Ramagundam, Peddapalli, Manchiryal, Manthani, Chennur and nearby areas.

What is the service charge for RO purifier?

General service: ₹300 Deep service: ₹500 Installation: ₹500 Repair charges depend on the issue.

Do you provide original RO filters and spare parts?

Yes, we provide 100% original filters, membranes, pumps, and all internal parts with warranty.

How often should I service my RO purifier?

RO should be serviced every 4–6 months to maintain good water quality and increase the purifier’s lifespan.

Do you repair all types of water purifiers?

Yes, we repair all brands — Kent, Aquaguard, Livpure, Aquafresh, Pureit, AO Smith, and all customized RO systems.

Do you sell new RO water purifiers?

Yes, we sell high-quality RO, UV, UF, and alkaline water purifiers with installation and warranty support.

How do I know when my RO filters need replacement?

Common signs: low water flow, bad taste, odor, or high TDS. You can also contact us for a free filter checkup.

Do you provide TDS checking service?

Yes, we check TDS levels using a digital TDS meter and suggest the best filter replacements based on your water quality.

How can I book a service or installation?

You can book a service by calling us or contacting us on WhatsApp. We offer same-day service in most locations.
